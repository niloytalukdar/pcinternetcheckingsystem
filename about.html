About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a reliable, real-time PC monitoring solution that empowers educational institutions and organizations to maintain seamless connectivity. By leveraging advanced PHP and MySQL integration, we aim to simplify laboratory management and enhance productivity for students, faculty, and staff.

Our Vision

We envision a future where technology streamlines classroom operations, ensuring every PC is monitored efficiently. Our goal is to expand our platform to support diverse environments, fostering a connected and productive academic community through innovative tools and data-driven insights.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of developers, designers, and educators from Premier University, passionate about harnessing technology to solve real-world challenges. Our collaborative efforts have resulted in the PUC PC_Monitor system, designed to provide a user-friendly and efficient solution for monitoring 120 PCs across four labs. We are committed to continuous improvement and community engagement.
